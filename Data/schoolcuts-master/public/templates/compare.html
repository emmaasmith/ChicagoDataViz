About this project

This website presents data from several sources, including the Chicago Public Schools, the Illinois State Board of Education, and the University of Chicago Urban Education Institute. Our goal is to help families with school children, other Chicago residents, and policy makers access and understand data that will help make informed decisions about the future of public schools.

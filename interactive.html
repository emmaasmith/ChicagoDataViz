Below is an interactive visualization of the precincts of Chicago.
The initial data shown is colored by % vote for Obama; it is plotted as % Emanuel vote in 2011 (Y-axis) by % Emanuel vote in 2015 (X-axis).
The dots on the map are schools affected by the 2013 public school closings. White stroke = closed school, black stroke = designated receiving school.
Below the visualizations are toggles to change the axes' variables, the variable affecting color, and to alter the range of each variable. The color is calculated as a linear scale.
